Cadenza is a MIDI performance manager for live shows. It sits between your MIDI instruments (keyboards, drum pads, guitars, etc.) and your synthesizers and controls all of the patches and their mappings. This allows you to create presets with arbitrary splits and layers in the software without worrying about how to program your particular hardware.

Cadenza was created to manage the large number of keyboard patches used in many musicals, but is well-suited for live performances of any kind.
